The Museum of Nature
Dinosaurs in a castle. Need we say more?

Hintonburg
Stroll through this up-and-coming neighbourhood and dine along Ottawa's restaurant row.

Byward Market
A popular nightlife and shopping destination. With dozens of bars, restaurants and quaint shops, it's a great place to stroll.

Gatineau Park
Hike and swim in this glorious wilderness park, just 30 minutes outside the city.

Le Nordik Spa
Experience alternate hot and cold baths for an extra kick.

Wakefield
Drive 30 minutes north and find yourself in an idyllic village. Be sure to head to the legendary Black Sheep Inn for great local folk music acts.
